Hello there, in this website I'll be giving you some tips to make your storytelling & character design much more easy and fun. P.S. this website is used to tell fantasy storys but feel free to use even if not.

tip 1# color palette - make sure your character doesnt blend in with the bg.

tip 2# color palette - lets say your palette is full of pastels so tr to give it one neon color where you want the viewers eyes to be drawn.

tip 3# character design - make sure your character is wearing something that is weared commonly from where they came from.

tip 4# character basics - lets say, for example, that your character came from a tribe that lives in a swamp. so your character might be intrested in tecnology.

tip 5# world history & rules - before even starting your story you should think about the world your story is being told: think about any major events that happend that changed the planet and some "common sence" rules in this world.
